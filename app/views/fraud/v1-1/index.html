Report MOT fraud

If you think someone is committing MOT fraud, you can report it.

What is MOT fraud?

MOT fraud happens when a person or a business breaks the rules for carrying out MOT tests on purpose, to cut corners or make profit.

They could be issuing MOT certificates for vehicles that haven’t been tested or deliberately passing vehicles that should have failed.

How to help

Please give as much information as possible, for example:

  • a detailed description of the problem
  • name and address of any person or business involved
  • information about any vehicles involved, for example registration numbers

Everything you provide will be anonymous.

What happens

DVSA will investigate the report.

If there is enough evidence they may issue a warning or suspension from MOT testing.

Appeal an MOT test

If you’re unhappy with the result of an individual MOT test, do not report it here. Appeal your MOT.
