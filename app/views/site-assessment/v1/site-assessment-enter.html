Vehicle Testing Station Enter site review

Premises

Clues for premises?

The VTS is a safe working environment where the test bay is clearly defined and MOT equipment is easily accessible and in good order

The equipment is calibrated and there is a system to ensure that calibrations are planned and carried out in a timely fashion

The MOT test bay and site meet the Requirements of Authorisation

Management Control and Quality Checks

Clues for Management Control and Quality Checks?

The VTS staff has a clear understanding of their testing responsibilities and security requirements relevant to the MOT service

The AE has an effective Quality Management system that is appropriate to its operation and would identify and rectify failures or weaknesses in test quality

The VTS demonstrates a positive culture of quality, communication and improvement

People

Clues for people?

The AE has a stable and well trained work force. New recruits are vetted and undergo an appropriate introductory regime and suprvision.

All mandatory training requirements are managed and systems are in place to ensure standards in testing and customer service is maintained

There are appropriate policies in place to encourage integrity in operation and support to MOT staff
